RGR FM Privacy Policy

The RGR FM & RGR Oldies application for Android may require certain permissions to run.

#1. Android Permissions:

None of the permissions will be used to capture and store information besides what’s mentioned in #2.

  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: To determine whether the device is connected to the internet to improve reliability of media streaming.
  • ACCESS_WIFI_STATE: To determine whether the device is connected to the internet to improve reliability of media streaming.
  • INTERNET: To stream music, a valid internet connection is required.
  • READ_PHONE_STATE: To detect incoming phone calls to stop music playback.

#2. Data

Data could be captured for diagnostics purposes to improve the application, such as:

  • Crash logs
  • Device
  • Device screen density
  • Device screen size
  • Locale

#3. Sharing with third parties

No data will be shared with third parties.
